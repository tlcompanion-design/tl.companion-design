App Features & Systems Explained

The core of the app is the Character Builder. This system lets you theorycraft and design your character from the ground up, providing a powerful visual interface to manage every aspect of your build.

  • Weapons & Armor: Equip any combination of main and off-hand weapons. Fill all your armor slots with gear of varying rarities and stats.
  • Traits & Runes: Fine-tune your gear by selecting specific traits and socketing powerful runes to unlock hidden potential.
  • Saved Builds: All your creations are saved to your account, allowing you to access and tweak them anytime, anywhere.

A great build is more than just gear. Our app lets you select and manage your active and passive skills to match your playstyle.

  • Skill Selection: Choose from a complete list of available skills for your chosen weapons.
  • Synergy Planning: Visualize how your skills, gear bonuses, and traits will work together in combat.

Instantly see the impact of your choices. Our precise CP Calculator updates in real-time as you adjust your build, removing the guesswork from character progression.

  • Real-Time Calculation: The total CP is automatically calculated based on your equipped items, selected traits, skill levels, runes, and more.
  • Detailed Breakdown: Understand exactly where your power comes from. The calculator shows how much CP is contributed by each categoryâ€”weapons, armor, skills, etc.
  • Informed Decisions: Compare different items or trait choices side-by-side to determine which upgrade gives you the most significant power boost.

Your data is secure, private, and always accessible. We use modern, secure backend services to manage your account and builds.

  • Secure Authentication: Sign in safely with Google or a traditional email/password login. Your account information is protected.
  • Cloud Storage: Your builds are stored in the cloud with Firebase Firestore, ensuring they are automatically backed up and synced across devices.
  • Privacy Control: Choose to keep your builds private for personal use or make them public to share with the community.
